About Us


Who we are

Trimex Distributors (Private) limited is owned by Zimbabweans and was founded in the year 2008 with the objective of becoming a major Supplier of Hospital Equipment, Surgical, Sundries, Dental, X-Ray Films, Physiotherapy, Laboratory Consumables Cleaning Materials, Stationery, Vehicles Parts and General Supplies

Vision

Our vision is to become a major business partner with most Organisations in Zimbabwe such as the Zimbabwean Government,NGOs and the Private Sector in Zimbabwe and beyond.

Values

  • We only undertake work that falls within our scope of service and expertise
  • We ensure that all staff members are adequately trained,competent & skilled
  • Avoid risk to staff and customers who procure products from us through dealing in potent commodities
  • We shall have no dealings with stolen merchandise; our actions are based on a mutually committed spirit on friendliness and honesty

more Values

  • Our relations to customers and business partners are oriented towards a common sustained benefit.
  • Our Corporate culture encourages trust and supports employees' development for achieving agreed targets.
  • we will deliver quality product to our customers who are the reason for our existence.
